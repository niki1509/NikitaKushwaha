open source ventilator projects

As we all know by now, ventilators are an important tool to help patients who are struggling to breath on their own. Modern ventilators are fairly sophisticated devices especially from a software standpoint, but at their heart, they are relatively simple mechanical devices. While there are several modes these devices can operate in, their end goal is to deliver a given amount of air to the patient and attempt to generate an ideal pressure and airflow curve.
